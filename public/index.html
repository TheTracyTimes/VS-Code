Our Mission

To be a City of Refuge where people can find hope, healing, and a relationship with Jesus Christ. We are committed to worshiping God, teaching His Word, and serving our community with love and compassion. To be a pillar in the house of the Lord is a vision every Christian should carry, as we learn to dwell continually in His presence together. As the Body of Christ, it is vital to understand what it means to be in the world and not of the world. Learning to stand apart, lead boldly, and press on faithfully until Jesus returns. We welcome you with the love of Christ into a place of safety and growth. Welcome to the City of Refuge, where you can set your anchor.
