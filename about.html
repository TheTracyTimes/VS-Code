About Us

A City of Refuge | A Community of Faith

Our History

Sarasota Gospel Temple has been a beacon of faith and hope in the community, dedicated to spreading the Gospel and serving those in need.

Founded on the principles of love, faith, and service, our church has grown into a vibrant community where believers come together to worship God, support one another, and make a positive impact in our community.

We are proud to be a place where families can grow spiritually, where the Word of God is taught with clarity and conviction, and where everyone is welcomed with open arms.

Our Mission

"To be a City of Refuge where people can find hope, healing, and a relationship with Jesus Christ. We are committed to worshiping God, teaching His Word, and serving our community with love and compassion."
