Our Ministries

Discover ways to grow, serve, and connect

Sarasota Gospel is committed to building a strong, and spiritual assembly. With this aim in mind, our ministries were founded in hopes of supporting our saints to grow as true Christ followers and in doing so, that we may be pillars in the community to reach out and help others. Learn more about each ministry below.

Youth Ministry

At Sarasota Gospel Temple, we recognize that the strength of our youth today is indicative of the success of the church of tomorrow. Therefore, at SGT we are committed to building young people that are spiritually-minded, biblically sound, and socially conscious in hopes that they will be strong Christian leaders each within their respective spheres. To support this mission, SGT provides many activities and outreach opportunities specifically tailored to the interests and concerns of our young people.

Prayer Ministry

We are a church who believes in the power of prayer. In Ephesians 6, apostle Paul encourages us to pray with all supplication and in the spirit, and this is what we strive to do both individually as saints of God, and collectively as a people of God. Prayer is our means of communication with God and by which we are strengthened in him. We urge all saints to cultivate a strong prayer life. Prayer services are held weekly by region to encourage saints to come together in one mind to pray and seek the Lord.

Bible Study

Sarasota Gospel offers weekly Bible studies to increase your understanding of scripture. Classes are divided by age and level understanding to ensure every saint is sufficiently exposed to the gospel and equipped in the word. Join us every Saturday night for bible study, where we dive into scripture and bible stories, and learn insights on how to apply them to our daily lives.

Music Ministry

Throughout scripture, music is mentioned as an essential part of worship and praise to God. The value and importance that God places on musical expression are clearly evidenced both in the Old and New Testaments. Here at SGT, we strongly support music's use in worship and believe it is a spiritual tool to connect with, and honor God. Our ensemble band and accompanying singers lead each of our services into praise and adoration. Additionally, we offer music classes for all levels and ages to those feeling called to music ministry. During the summer months, special music camps and intensives are also offered.
